MY FAVORITE FOOD IS DOSA

Dosa is one of the my favorite foods. beacause it is easy to make. it is perfect choice for breakfast. side of dosa will be very crispy and yummy. in tamilnadu dosa is very popular food. many varientes are there. like oninon dosa, podi dosa, egg dosa, masala dosa. my favorite is oninon dosa.

How to do dosa?

Let's Go!

Ingredients you need

  • Dosa rice flour
  • Sun flower oil or refined oil
  • Milagaai Podi for side dish

Steps to do dosa

  1. Take Dhava 🍳
  2. On the stove with fire 😀
  3. Put 2 or 3 drops oil 🥄
  4. Paste your flour in round shape 😋
  5. Wait for 1 minute 🙂
  6. Yeah, dosa is ready 🥰
